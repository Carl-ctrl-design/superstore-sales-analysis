# Superstore Retail Sales Performance Analysis

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel | PivotTables | Power Query | KPI Dashboard  
**Dataset:** Kaggle Superstore Sales Dataset (9,994 rows, 2014–2017)  
**Author:** Carlton Waiti  

## Executive Summary
This analysis evaluates the key drivers of revenue and profitability across a multi-region retail business.
Despite strong top-line revenue growth, the business is experiencing profit leakage driven by discounting practices and underperforming product lines.

The findings identify specific loss drivers and provide data-backed recommendations to improve margins, optimize pricing strategy, and refocus product priorities.

## Business Problem
A retail chain wanted to understand what was driving revenue and margin performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments and where the business was losing money despite high sales volumes.

## What I Analyzed
The analysis was structured across five key areas:
1. Product Performance: Revenue and profit by category and sub-category
2. Regional Analysis: Profitability comparison across regions
3. Discount Impact: Correlation between discount levels and profit margins
4. Customer Segmentation: Value and profitability by segment
5. Trend Analysis: Year-over-year sales performance (2014–2017)

## Key Findings
1. Hidden Loss Drivers in Product Mix
Tables and Bookcases generate high revenue but negative profit margins
Indicates structural issues in pricing or cost
2. Discounting is Destroying Profit
Discounts above 20% consistently result in losses
1,458 orders are loss-making due to excessive discounting
Profit margin drops from +29.5% (no discount) to -119% (high discount)
3. **Regional Profitability Imbalance**
   - Central region has the lowest margin (7.9%)
   - Despite being 3rd highest in revenue
   - Suggests inefficient pricing or aggressive discounting
4. Technology Category Dominates
Highest revenue AND highest profitability
Represents the core growth driver of the business
5. Customer Segment Value Gap
Corporate segment delivers higher margin per order
More valuable than Consumer segment in profitability terms
